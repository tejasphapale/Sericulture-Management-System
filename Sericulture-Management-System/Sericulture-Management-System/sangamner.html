About Sericulture in Sangamner

Sangamner is a leading hub for sericulture in Ahmednagar district, well-known for mulberry cultivation and cocoon production. The farmers here are dedicated to producing cocoons, silk yarn, and by-products that contribute significantly to Maharashtra’s silk industry.
