Disease Management

Effective strategies to protect mulberry plants from common diseases for a healthy and productive sericulture farm.

Introduction

Effective disease management ensures healthy mulberry plants, essential for successful silkworm rearing. Proper identification, prevention, and treatment of diseases maximize crop yield and quality. Our platform helps farmers adopt sustainable practices to manage diseases efficiently.

Key Aspects of Disease Management

Leaf Spot Disease

Caused by fungi, leaf spots appear as brown or black spots on leaves. Remove affected leaves and use recommended fungicides.

Powdery Mildew

White powdery coating on leaves affects photosynthesis. Apply sulfur-based fungicides and ensure proper air circulation.

Root Rot

Occurs in poorly drained soils. Use proper drainage techniques and apply fungicides to affected roots.

Bacterial Blight

Yellowing and wilting of leaves caused by bacteria. Remove infected plants and spray copper-based bactericides.
