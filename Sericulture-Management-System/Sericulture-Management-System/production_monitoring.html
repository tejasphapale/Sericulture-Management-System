Production Monitoring

Track and optimize the entire silkworm rearing and silk production process for maximum efficiency and yield.

Introduction

Production monitoring is essential for efficient sericulture management. It helps track every stage of silkworm rearing, leaf consumption, cocoon production, and overall farm performance. Our platform enables real-time data tracking, early identification of issues, and performance optimization for maximum productivity.

Key Aspects of Production Monitoring

Silkworm Growth Tracking

Monitor each instar stage of silkworms, track weight gain, and detect any growth abnormalities early.

Leaf Consumption Monitoring

Track daily leaf consumption to ensure healthy feeding rates and detect shortages or overfeeding patterns.

Cocoon Production Analysis

Analyze cocoon yield data per batch to evaluate productivity and silkworm health over time.

Performance Reports

Generate detailed reports summarizing farm performance metrics, helping in decision-making and process optimization.
